中国梦背景下，年轻人有更多机会实现自我价值
While striving for the Chinese Dream, young people enjoy more opportunities to realize their self-worth. As far as I am concerned, it is not only a gift from the new era but also a mission shouldered by the youth, inspiring us to realize our own goals.
Under the Chinese Dream, youngsters have abundant development opportunities. Firstly, teachnological advancement and industrial upgrading have created a large number of jobs in emerging fields such as AI and new energy, which provide a broad stage for young people. Secondly, various entrepreneurship subsidy policies pave the way for young people and help them achieve their career aspirations.Lastly, the state’s vigorous support for education in rural and remote areas has attracted many young people to devote themselves to this cause and pursue the careers they love.
As the hope of the new era, we should closely link personal dreams with the development of the country. In this way, we will not only achieve our own life goals but also contribute our wisdom and strength to the nation’s progress.

教师对学生的影响
It is believed that teachers can exert a profound influence on their students’academic pursuit and personal development. From my perspective, a good teacher is not only a transmitter of knowledge in the classroom but also a guide in students’journey of growth.
Academically, teachers play a key role in sparking students’interest in learning. A case in point is my English teacher, Ms.Liu. She always has ways to transform tedious knowledge into vivid stories, helping students learn English easy and happily. She also teaches effective learning methods, such as logical thinking and problem-solving skills, which lay a soild foundation for students’future academic research. More importantly, teachers set an example of integrity and responsibility through thrie daily words and behaviors. They encourage students to face challenges bravely and correct their mistakes with patience. Without the guide of teachers, students are very likely to lose their way.
There is no doubt that teachers’influence on students is both profound and lasting. Therefore, it will eventually accompany students throughout their long lives, becoming the most soild support on their path to growth.
